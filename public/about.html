About Me

I am just an old lady trying to make it into this world.

Once upon a time in the whimsical town of Quirkville, there lived a spry old lady named Gertrude McTicklepants. At the ripe age of 92, Gertrude had more energy than a caffeinated squirrel on a pogo stick.

Every morning, she would don her neon pink jumpsuit with matching feather boa and rollerblade her way to the local bingo hall, leaving a trail of glitter and giggles in her wake. The other bingo players would stare in amazement as Gertrude gracefully skated around the tables, calling out numbers with the enthusiasm of a cheerleader at a pom-pom convention.

Her secret to longevity? Gertrude avoided other people like the plague.

So do me a favour and let me be like Gertrude.
